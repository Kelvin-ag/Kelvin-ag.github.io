Washington EV Analysis | Tableau

This interactive dashboard built in Tableau provides a comprehensive overview of electric vehicle (EV) adoption across counties in Washington State. The analysis explores trends in vehicle types, manufacturer market share, geographic distribution, and adoption over time.
The dashboard highlights key insights such as the dominance of Battery Electric Vehicles (BEVs) and strong manufacturer presence led by Tesla. Visualisations allow users to explore the top counties for EV ownership, identify regional adoption clusters through a postcode-level map, and examine year-by-year growth in EV registrations.
